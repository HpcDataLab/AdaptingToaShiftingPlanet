## Repository relationship to the paper

This repository provides standardized occurrence CSV files, metadata, and generic scripts for reproducing the species distribution modeling workflow described in the paper. The complete environmental and modeling data archive is deposited in Zenodo under DOI https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20631938.
